WELCOME TO PUZZLE MANIA!

The objective of the game is to complete the puzzle.

To play this game, select pieces from above the grid and click on the grid to place the piece.

For every three pieces you place incorrectly, you lose a life.

The image must form using the pieces will be shown initially and then the pieces will be removed,
leaving you with an empty grid.

It is up to you to solve the puzzle in order to retrieve the flashlight from Planet Puzzle Mania!

Good luck!
